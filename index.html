DEDSEC

A secret hacking collective featured in the Watch Dogs series. They have a worldwide presence and millions of followers, with their goal being to expose the corruption in the common world, including large corporations.

BLUME CORPORATION

The communications technology company that developed CTOS as well as its iterations, and is responsible for their operation, maintenance and advancement.

UMENI-ZULU

better known as Umeni Security Corps or Umeni Corporation, is a private security company and faction.

PRIME_EIGHT

Led by Lenni, and are the rival hacker group to DedSec, whom DedSec is trying to take down. They are amoral mercenaries (comparable to the Fixers) who sell data and exploits to anyone who can pay them.
